The Modern Pack

Our most sought-after package, offering a comprehensive rejuvenation for your vehicle, inside and out. Experience a deep clean that restores your car's showroom appeal.

  • Full Interior & Exterior Detailing
  • Tire & Rim Deep Clean
  • Streak-Free Window Cleaning (Interior & Exterior)
  • Pet Hair Removal (if needed)
  • Carpet & Upholstery Shampoo/Steam
  • Protective Sealant Application
  • Maintenance Check & Treatment

The Disaster Plan

When your vehicle has seen better days, our Disaster Plan steps in. This intensive package is designed for cars in extremely rough condition, requiring extensive time and specialized techniques.

  • Heavy-Duty Interior Deep Clean
  • Stain & Odor Removal
  • Headliner Cleaning
  • Comprehensive Exterior Restoration
  • Extended Service Time

Please Note: Prices for all services, especially the Disaster Plan, will be quoted upon inspection and may vary based on the vehicle's condition and severity of dirt/damage. **Please ensure water and power access is available at the detailing location.**
